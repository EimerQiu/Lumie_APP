FAQ

Answers for teens,
parents, and clinicians

Do I need the ring to use Lumie?

No. You can start with the app alone and add the ring later for deeper biometric insights.

Can my parents see all my data?

Only what you choose to share. All sharing is opt-in and can be revoked instantly.

How do I revoke family access?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Family ‚Üí Leave Family. Your data is immediately unshared.

Is this a medical device?

No. Lumie is for wellness tracking and education‚Äînot diagnosis or treatment.

Why can't users under 13 register?

We comply with COPPA and design specifically for teens 13‚Äì21 with chronic conditions.

What if I switch phones?

All data syncs to the cloud. Just log in on your new device.

Does Lumie work offline?

Core features like logging and reminders work offline. Syncs when back online.

Can I use Lumie for multiple conditions?

Yes. Select all condition groups that apply to you.

Why we built Lumie

Growing up with a chronic condition shouldn't mean growing up with shame, fear, or constant comparison.

Lumie was created to give teens living with chronic conditions a sense of understanding and control. Our team believes in teen-first design, non-clinical language, and creating tools that empower rather than label.

We work with healthcare advisors, patient advocates, and most importantly‚Äîteens themselves‚Äîto build something genuinely helpful, not just another health app.
